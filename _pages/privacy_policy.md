# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how appsinacup.com ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and any apps published by appsinacup ("apps").

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information through our website and apps:
- **Personal Information:** Only if you voluntarily provide it (e.g., via contact forms, email, or within our apps). This may include your name, email address, or other contact details.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect non-personal information about your interaction with the site or apps, such as IP address, device type, operating system, browser type, pages/screens visited, and time spent. This is typically collected via analytics tools and cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Operate and maintain the website and apps
- Respond to your inquiries
- Improve site and app functionality and user experience
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends

## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and apps may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site or apps may not function properly.

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only as required by law or to protect our rights, for both the website and our apps.

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information across our website and apps. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Links

Our website and apps may contain links to external sites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

## Children's Privacy

Our website and apps are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Updates apply to both the website and all apps published by appsinacup.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our apps, please contact us at contact@appsinacup.com.
