## Overview

**Gonuts** is a custom build of Godot 4.5 preconfigured with additional platforms and plugins:
- [tiny_lobby](https://github.com/appsinacup/addon_tiny_lobby_client): Multiplayer lobbies with real-time Lua-powered logic.
- [GodotSteam](https://godotsteam.com): Integrate with Steam.
- [DiscordEmbeddedApp](https://discord.com/developers/docs/developer-tools/embedded-app-sdk): Integrate with Discord Embedded App.

Work in progress:
- [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com): Embedding the vscode in the editor.
